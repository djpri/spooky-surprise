# Factions

- The Brichan
  - Role: Vowed lightbearers balancing grace and geis.
  - Goal: Keep Alderthorn whole by cleansing, binding, or devouring spirits.
  - Tension: Every victory costs memory and deepens corruption.

- Sisterhood of the Pyre (Sister Maerwyn)
  - Role: Keepers of communal pyres and mirror‑rites.
  - Goal: Maintain ritual order; keep panic from spreading during Year’s End.
  - Tension: Doubt in whether the gods still watch.

- Alderthornians (Villagers)
  - Role: The living; caretakers of anchors, fields, and dead.
  - Goal: Survive the Hunt; protect families and names.
  - Tension: Fear turns to blame when rites fail.

- The Archivist of Ash
  - Role: Custodian of the record in a liminal archive.
  - Goal: Preserve continuity of Hunts; seek release from duty.
  - Tension: Truth vs mercy — the record may demand your death.

- The Voice Beneath the Altar
  - Role: Breach intelligence; source or mirror of corruption.
  - Goal: Intimates release, union, or collapse — agenda unclear.
  - Tension: Temptation of power vs annihilation.

- Old Ways and New Faith (Metaphysical)
  - Role: Talamh (root and return) vs Nèamh (judgement and breath); Muir mediates.
  - Goal: Balance light, reflection, and root flame.
  - Tension: Extremes tear the Veil; balance is the narrow path.
